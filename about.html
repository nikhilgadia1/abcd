"1 Day - 24 Hours - 24 Events - 24 Missions - ∞ Talents"

Past

The timeline is a timeless concept, an infinitely stretching, infinitely repeating stream filled with a myriad of parallel realities flowing simultaneously along the time-stream. For eons, He Who Remains, the guardian of the Sacred Timeline, has warded off threats like Variants and Nexus Beings whose very existence threatens the collapse of the universe. With the assassination of He Who Remains, the timeline has fallen into disrepair and its uncontrollable branching risks the end of all life as we know it. The burden of its protection now rests on your shoulders.

Present

Your mission, if you choose to accept it, is to prevent the timeline from self-destructing and eliminating all of creation. We have identified a set of 24 inter-dimensional critical nexus points, all of whom pose threats to the sanctity of the timeline. Assemble your team of elite multidimensional warriors to tackle these nexus points one-by-one and prevent the space-time continuum for imploding on itself. Be warned, trespassing through the timeline presents unimaginable dangers and infinitesimally small probabilities of success but that’s just a quick 20 minute adventure for you, right?

Future

Assuming all of you manage to return in one piece, of the same age and from the same universe, you shall be deemed Guardians of the Timeline and go down in history beside the likes of Loki and Doctor Strange as people who dared to risk it all for the sake of the timeline. Remember, your sole priority is to restore the timeline to its normal state and prevent it from collapsing and taking the multiverse with it. We invite you to test your abilities in this almost impossible mission that transcends the confines of space and time and see if the odds are in your favor or not.
Godspeed, brave tributes.
