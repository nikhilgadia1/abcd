Bosco Brainchild - Eco Fashion & Interior Decor

They say a magician never reveals his secrets, so weave your magic through the intricate tapestry of the ecosystem and conceive solutions to climate change. Your ideas may just hold the power to stop Armageddon and prevent the collapse of civilization as we know it. Use your ingenuity and brainstorm solutions for problems to be faced by 31st Century humans (if we survive another millenia). This is your opportunity to determine whether our future will be a utopia or a dystopia.


Rules

THEME: CLIMATE CHANGE AND SUSTAINABLE SOLUTIONS OVER TIME

Introduction: BOSCO BRAINCHILD#creations 2024 eco-fashion & interior décor is an E-AI (Environmental Artistic Intelligence) that defines a system of capacities for perception, sensing, discernment, insight, activity, eco-friendly, sustainable solutions and divergent synthesis that is developed by and transcends beyond human intelligence. It identifies four creative intelligence styles that include imaginative, intuitive, inspirational, and innovative for sustainable solutions engaging in old and used items to new upcycled fashion and décor items.

No. of participants: 2

Duration: 120 minutes

  • List of materials:
    • Clothes
    • Plastic bottles
    • Tin cans
    • Newspaper/magazines
    • Earthen pots
    • Cartons
    • Egg cartons
    • Sawdust/wood pieces
    • Junk items
    • Batteries
    • Keys
    • Wool
    • Scotch Brite
    • Body scrubber
    • Shirt hanger
    • Bags
    • Toothbrush
    • Comb
    • Coconut shell
  • Rules:
    • Participants must submit a hardcopy mentioning items being used to the judges on the day of the event.
    • Old to new upcycled item/s in keeping with sustainable solutions.
    • Participants must bring their own requirements (adhesive, scissor, cello tape, double-sided tape, stapler, needle & thread, buttons, paints, paint-brush, palette, sequences etc.)
    • IMPORTANT: SUPERGLUE (Fevi kwik) and SPRAY PAINTS will NOT be permitted.
    • Participants must clean their workstation after completion of the event.
    • Participants must take back all unused materials that were brought by them.
    • NOTE: No material(s) will be provided by the host school.
    • Late submission will incur a deduction of points.
    • Both participants are required to actively participate in the event for its entire duration.
    • A bell will ring at the end of 120 minutes and all participants MUST stop.
    • The participants must demonstrate the utility of their products within a time frame of 3+2 minutes.
    • No digital means of presenting the utility of the product shall be allowed.
    • The judges’ decision will be binding on all the participants.
    • Littering the campus is strictly prohibited.
    • Participants are STRICTLY PROHIBITED from posting any images/videos on social media during the fest.
