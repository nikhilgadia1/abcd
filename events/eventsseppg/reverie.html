Bosco Reverie - Creative Writing

Get your ‘time machine’ and step into a new timeline where the past, present and future converge in a symphony of prose crafting. Let us embark on a creative writing extravaganza and get Doraemon’s ‘Anywhere Door’ to hop on a writing adventure, where every page turn will take you to endless possibilities. Prepare your wits and pick up your pens for a day of uproarious wordplay and refined creativity in this war of words.


Rules

Participants: 3 (Individual)

Duration: 60 minutes

  • The participants will be provided with topics on the day of the Fest. Participants are granted the liberty to choose their own subject matter.
  • There will be topics pertaining to 3 different languages- English, Hindi and Bengali. The schools must have 3 participants, each ready to compose a topic in one of the 3 different languages.
  • Participants are expected to write their compositions within 500 - 1000 words for English and Bengali and in 1000 words for Hindi.
  • Participants will be provided with a pen and paper. No other equipment, especially electronics, will be allowed.
  • The usage of dialogues and catch-phrases in the story, akin to the topic is encouraged.
  • The judges will evaluate the submissions on the quality of the written material, structure of the story, description, imagery and any other criteria they deem necessary.
  • Usage of vulgarity, profanity or inappropriate language will not be allowed and will lead to the submission being void.
  • Utilisation of unethical methods will result in immediate disqualification of the participant.
