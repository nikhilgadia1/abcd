Rules

Vibrant Hues and Green Tee shirts

THEME: CLIMATE CHANGE OVER TIME

Introduction: BOSCO FRESCO#creations 2024 Vibrant Hues and Green tee shirts. E-AI (Environmental Artistic Intelligence) that defines a spectrum of capabilities with conception, insight, activity, eco-friendly choices and divergent synthesis of creativity. Engaging in showcasing Green to Grey on Canvas and an old Tee shirt.

No. of participants: 2

Duration: 120 minutes

List of materials: Canvas (A3 size) and an old Tee shirt (Regular size).

Tee shirts can be of any self-colour, eco-friendly material, well used and fit for refurbishing to an upcycled fashion item.

  • Paints (Non-Toxic)
  • Palette
  • Twigs
  • Sequences
  • Markers
  • Cotton
  • Leaves
  • Thread
  • Colour pencils
  • Cleaning Cloth
  • Flowers
  • Feather
  • Paintbrushes
  • Wool
  • Jute
  • Scissors
  • Toothbrush
  • Vegetables
  • Buttons

Rules:

  • Participants MUST submit a hardcopy mentioning items being used to the judges on the day of the event.
  • Old to new upcycled Tee shirt and Expressive Canvas painting.
  • The paintings MUST correlate with the theme.
  • The frontal of the tee shirt MUST depict the pre-industrial era and the back MUST depict the post-industrial era relative to the theme.
  • Painting on the canvas MUST depict both pre-industrial and post-industrial eras relative to the theme.
  • Only A3 size Canvas will be provided by the Host school.
  • Participants must bring their own requirements from the list of materials.
  • All stationery and artistic mediums must be eco-friendly.
  • Any sort of tracing or duplicating will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Borrowing is strictly prohibited.
  • IMPORTANT: SUPERGLUE (Fevi kwik) and SPRAY PAINTS will NOT be permitted.
  • Any kind of Vulgarity depicted through the artwork will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Participants must clean their workstation after completion of the event.
  • Participants must take back all unused materials that were brought by them.
  • NOTE: No material(s) will be provided by the host school.
  • Late submission will incur a deduction of points.
  • Both participants are required to actively participate in the event for its entire duration.
  • A bell will ring at the end of 120 minutes and all participants MUST stop.
  • Judges will question the participants on their artwork and its relevance to the theme. Time: 3+2 minutes.
  • No digital means of presenting the relevance of the theme will be allowed.
  • The judges’ decision will be final and binding on all the participants.
  • Littering the campus is strictly prohibited.
  • Participants are STRICTLY PROHIBITED from posting any images/videos on social media during the fest.
