Bosco Premiere - Short Film

Lights, Camera and Action!! Bring your movie-making magic to the silver screen, give the audience a hair-raising, edge of the seat experience like Rambo and John Wick or baffle the masses with a Nolanesque thriller, making generations debate if the top is still spinning or not. Will you leave your beloved watchers devastated over Iron Man’s death or hanging off the edge of a cliff wondering why Katappa killed Bahubali?


Rules

Participants: 5+10

Movie duration: 5-10 minutes approx.

All schools must register as a team, having a minimum of 5 and a maximum of 15 participants, including:

  • a director
  • a scriptwriter
  • a cinematographer
  • an editor
  • the actors
  • It should be of an appropriate quality, pertaining to the theme given to the schools.
  • The storyline of the film should be an original one and should not be plagiarised from any existing project.
  • Each school must give an introduction to the film, before the film starts.
  • The time duration of the film will include the introduction and the credits.
  • Exceeding the given time limit will result in penalisation.
  • The theme of the film for each school will be given on 27th June, 2024 and the made film must be submitted by 3rd July, 2024 to home.boscofest@gmail.com.
  • No part of the film should endorse any form of vulgarity or obscenity.
  • The script should be predominantly in English. Vernacular phrases might be used. Excessive use of vernacular language as well as visual effects will lead to penalisation.
  • Any appropriate type of music which is available on the internet, or, self-composed, can be included occasionally.
  • Top 5 schools’ submissions shall be screened.
