Bosco Tribunal - Moot Court

"Don't play the odds, play the man.” Even if you can survive the wrath of time, Law will still come for you. Can't believe it? Ask Loki. In the corridors of time, you are left with nothing but proofs and witnesses. In the end, all that stays is a court of opinion and the only way to “win a no-win situation is by rewriting the rules.”


Rules

Participants: 2

Duration: 3+2 minutes

Round 1:

  • The event will be conducted in the traditional moot court format. No evidence or witness will be admitted.
  • The cases will be given 24 hours before the event.
  • Total time per team will be 3+2 minutes, two minutes for rebuttal.
  • The two paired schools will be allotted a lawsuit, each team representing the plaintiff or the defendant, based on a draw of chits the day of the reps meet.
  • Use of precedents is highly recommended.
  • All arguments presented and precedents cited need to be in accordance with Indian law and pertaining statutes. Proper court etiquette must be followed at all times.
  • The use of unparliamentary language and unruly behaviour will not be entertained and may result in disqualification of the offending team.
  • Top 4 schools from Round 1 will be qualifying into Round 2. Rules for Round 2 will be disclosed on the spot.
