Bosco Maverick - Unconventional Games

The first rule of fight club is that… there are no rules?? As Dante said, ‘Abandon all hope ye who enter’ for once you take a step into this hellish realm of confusion and absolute lawlessness, there is no going back. Even the laws of physics are scared of this vortex of madness where anything and everything goes. So, do you dare to gamble your sanity for a shot at glory?


Rules

    Rounds: 3

    Participants: 2

    Rules:

    • The rounds are designed to test the coordination, intellectual prowess and endurance of the participants.
    • There shall be no system of elimination in these rounds and the winner will solely be decided upon the summation of the 3 rounds altogether.
    • The rest of the rules shall unravel with time....
