1 minute, 60 seconds, Infinite possibilities. You only get one shot, one chance to make your mark on history and either be eternally immortalized or instantaneously forgotten. Every passing second in this event is a chance to turn it all around. Race against the ruthless sands of time and emerge victorious in this grand vesture. So ‘Carpe Diem’, my friends.


Rules

BOSCO BLITZ (Just a Minute) - Science

Participants: 1

Duration: 60 seconds + 2 minutes

  • All participants shall be given 60 seconds, i.e., 1 minute, to speak on a topic previously decided by the organisers.
  • The topic to be spoken shall be given to them 5 seconds before commencement.
  • During the speech, the use of vulgarity or profanity shall be heavily downmarked by the judges.
  • If the student exceeds the limit, 0.5 points shall be deducted for every 1 second exceeded.
  • After the initial speech of the participants, an interpellation round shall follow in which they will be asked to defend their respective points on the topic to be asked by the judges present.
