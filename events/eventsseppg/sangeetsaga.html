Bosco Sangeet Saga - Antakshari

Gear up for a musical time-travel extravaganza. If Ed Sheeran can groove to Punjabi beats and Enrique Iglesias can serenade in Hindi, then why not join us in diving into the kaleidoscopic universe of Bollywood melodies? From the timeless classics of 'Chalte Chalte’ to the foot-tapping beats of 'Chikni Chameli,' let's journey through the eras of melody. It's a rendezvous where the past, present, and future collide in a harmonious symphony of tunes.


Rules

Participants: 3

Duration: 120 mins

  • Only Hindi songs will be allowed.
  • Singing songs containing vulgar lyrics will lead to disqualification.
  • There will be an elimination round consisting of three rounds, and the top 8 teams shall be selected for the next level.
  • All teams will participate in the preliminary round.
  • Further rules specific to the different rounds will be mentioned during the event.
  • Questions will be based on Hindi movies and audio/visual clips. Some of the rounds will also have negative markings.
  • Bonus rounds will also be there.
  • The game master's decision will be final and binding.
  • All participating schools are informed that there will be a retro round from the 1960’s onwards.
