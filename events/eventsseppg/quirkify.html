Bosco Quirkify - Ad Spoof

Picture a timeless classic like the Coca-Cola ad, but with polar bears wearing VR headsets and doing the floss dance. Or perhaps a nod to the iconic "Mac vs. PC" campaign, reimagined with a time-traveling twist where PCs from the past clash with futuristic Macs. Here, the skip ad button is only a cowardly escape and where you can use all the quirky references you need to remove the saying, “Ads kaun dekhta hai”.


Rules

Participants: 5+2 (for technical assistance)

Duration: 5+2 minutes (including setup)

  • Each school will be allotted a unique scenario and they have to come up with an effective ad spoof to positively impact their influence, brand value, reach, etc.
  • The schools must get their own props for the acts.
  • English shall be the only medium of communication during the course of the event.
  • Obscenity, abusive language, vulgarity, of any kind will lead to disqualification.
  • Topics will be chosen by the school reps by a draw of chits on the day of the rep meet.
