Bosco Polemic - Debate

“Don't raise your voice, improve your argument” Step into the intellectual colosseum where words wield power and arguments are forged in the fires of reason. Feel the tension in the air as the clock ticks down, driving each contender toward their ultimate destiny. In this arena, wit is your weapon, and logic your shield, as you battle for supremacy in the realm of ideologies. You must maintain your stand the way Ross did when he said, “We were on a break!”


Rules

Participants: 2

Duration: 3+3+1+1 minutes

  • There will be 2 participants from each school. One will speak ‘for’ and the other ‘against’ the motion.
  • A preparation time of 30 minutes will be provided to the participants.
  • 3+3 minutes of speech, both for and against the motion, and 1 minute each for rebuttal will be provided to the participants.
  • Reading out from any references shall be strictly penalised.
  • Use of any kind of profanity or vulgarity during the speeches by the participants shall lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Any such decision taken by the judges shall be final.
